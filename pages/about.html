About Me

Hello I'm Kevin Albarran and I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer currently based in the LA area. I got my start in web development after attending a coding bootcamp after college.




I started out working in the customer service industry (Trader Joes if you are a fan) and got my first professional start working at a telecommunications engineering company.




I always like to highlight the work I did at Trader Joes because it ended up teaching me a lot about working in changing environments and allowed me to see similar correlations that exist in the physical world and the software world. This also really helped develop many of my soft skills since 90% of the job relied on having good communication skills.
